The best k-dramas according to me

My top 5 k-dramas of all time

Crash Landing on You

Funny drama of an heiress accidentally ending up in North Korea

Nevertheless

A story about the complex and toxic relationships of art students. Amazing original soundtrack

Itaewon Class

Touching drama about a small business fighting people of money and power, each character in their own way

Start Up

Heartwrenching tale revolving around a Start-Up buisness competition

Our Blues

Beautiful drama about the different perspectives of the lives of all the characters living on Jeju
